Sample Lesson: Writing a Strong Grant Narrative

Essential Question

How can I clearly communicate my program idea in a way that funders understand and support?

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

  • Identify the purpose of a grant narrative.
  • Write a clear problem statement.
  • Connect program goals to measurable outcomes.

Mini Lesson

A strong grant narrative explains the need, the solution, the people served, and the expected results. It should be clear, specific, and aligned to the funder’s priorities.
